Associate in Computer Technology

Program Description:

A two year ladderized program leading to a diploma in "Associate in Computer Technology" which provides knowledge and skills in the fundamentals of computer programming and basic computation.

Program Objectives:

1. Equip students with specific skills for entry into the information technology profession.

2. Prepare the students for eventual entry into regular degree programs BSIT or BSCS.

Career Opportunities:

1. Computer Programmer
3. Computer Technician
4. Data Encoder
5. Graphic Designer
6. Web Developer
7. Office Assistant
8. Programmer Analyst
